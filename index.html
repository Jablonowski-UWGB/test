The Story in Me: Why Publishing is Important

Tiffany Jablonowski

Sometimes the story inside takes control of you, and until an escape becomes available the body and mind are at war!

Digital publishing provides a release valve for all people because it is
quick
easy
cost effective
 However this intercoonnection to the web can also be the ties that bind.

The inner peace of mind that sitting and reading a book gives me is magical. A book could take you on any adventure, and with digital publishing the options are limitless.

I would like to create my own stories, but my dream is to help others release their stories and ideas.

Whether creating an original story or helping others make their story come alive, publishing is is key for :
SHARING
COLLABORATING
EDUCATING
UNITING
